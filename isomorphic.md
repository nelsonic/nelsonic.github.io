What is Isomorphism?

> In mathematics, an isomorphism (from the Greek: ἴσος isos "equal", and μορφή morphe "shape")
> is a homomorphism (or more generally a morphism) that **admits an inverse**.
> http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isomorphism
